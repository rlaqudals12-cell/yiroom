App Name: Yiroom - AI Beauty & Wellness
Short Description (80 chars): AI-powered personal color, skin, body & beauty analysis. Your beauty coach.

Full Description:

Know yourself, wholly. Yiroom.

Yiroom is an AI-powered beauty & wellness platform that helps you discover
your unique beauty profile. With just a photo, get comprehensive analysis
of your personal color, skin type, body shape, hair, and makeup style
with personalized care recommendations.

--- Key Features ---

[AI Beauty Analysis]
- Personal Color : Spring/Summer/Autumn/Winter + detailed sub-tones
- Skin Analysis : 6-point assessment (hydration, oil, sensitivity, pores, wrinkles, elasticity)
- Body Shape : Skeleton-based classification + styling guide
- Hair Analysis : Hair type, scalp condition, customized care routine
- Makeup Analysis : Face shape & tone-matched makeup recommendations
- Oral Health : Tooth color & gum health check

[Personalized Recommendations]
- Skincare routine designed from your skin analysis
- Cosmetics matched to your personal color palette
- Exercise & styling guide for your body type
- Weather-based outfit recommendations

[Track Your Journey]
- Analysis history to monitor changes
- Closet management + AI outfit styling
- Nutrition/exercise logging + AI wellness coach

[Social]
- Share beauty routines with friends
- Wellness challenges on the leaderboard
